About Project Y-Blood

y-Blood is a digital blood donor repository for the people of Panangangara. It maintains a secure database of names, addresses, and blood groups of Yuvadhara members and local residents.

The goal is to ensure that in times of medical emergency, a compatible donor can be found within seconds through our unified platform. Developed and Maintained by Shafeer Shan
