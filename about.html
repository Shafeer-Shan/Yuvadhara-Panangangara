OUR STORY

WHO WE ARE

Yuvadhara Panangangara is more than just a club; its a dedication to community. With over four decades of history, we are a group of passionate individuals committed to fostering growth through sports, arts, and educational initiatives. Our goal is to nurture talent, build lifelong connections, and create a positive, lasting impact within our locality.
