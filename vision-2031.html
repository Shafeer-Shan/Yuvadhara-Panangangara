Roadmap to Excellence

VISION 2031

Transforming Yuvadhara into a self-reliant, modern institution for the future of Panangangara.

Building a Permanent Legacy

Planned in December 2025, "Vision 2031" is not just a project; it is a promise. Our primary goal is to establish a 'self-owned club building' that serves as a hub for sports, culture, and community welfare.

  • Five-Year Strategic Implementation
  • Economic Independence Model
  • Infrastructure for the Next Generation

The Financial Commitment

₹1,00,000 / Year Contribution

To ensure success, every Executive Committee serving between 2026 and 2031 is committed to contributing a minimum of 'One Lakh Rupees annually' towards the building fund.

Total targeted economic corpus by 2031: ₹5,00,000+

2031 Objectives

01

Physical Standards

Construction of the Yuvadhara Headquarters with indoor training facilities and a modern library lounge.

02

Digital Hub

Transitioning all club administrative functions into a paperless, digital environment for total transparency.

03

Economic Autonomy

Developing sustainable revenue streams through sports facility rentals and community memberships.
